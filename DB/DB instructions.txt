1. Open the model file with MYSQL workbench
2. Once you have it open (you see the diagram) click on the top "Database" => "Synchronize Model"
3. Select local instance MySQL80 or whichever local connection you'd like to use. click next
4. ignore sync options, click next
5. put in your password for your selected database, click next
6. select mydb, click next
7. put in your password for your selected database, click next
8. "select changes to apply" should be set to update your local database with the new model. click next
9. click execute
10. put in your password for your selected databaseand click close. You've got a local instance of the database :D
